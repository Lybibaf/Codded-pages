About G.B.H.S BAFOUSSAM (LYBIBAF)

G.B.H.S Bafoussam is the oldest institute of Secondary Education in Bafoussam, established in 1956 by a French Cameroonian. It was named after this French Cameroonian. A statue stands today infront of the College's Yard, and is perhaps the Colleges best known Landmark.

View of the list of historical facts

G.B.H.S Bafoussam has had a good number buildings from the time of foundation till present where a number of projects on buildings is still going on. The college had grown from nine students with a single master to an enrollment of more than 2, 000 and 2, 500 graduates at the A-level and BACC respectively. At present the college has about 15, 000 students.

The Early times of G.B.H.S Bafoussam
At the very begining were the fresh moment of LYBIBAF. When the environment and its plants were still very young making it's beauty much more appreciable. As time passed on, Our environment and it's plants grew older nevertheless becoming more and more beautiful. Also, as time passed on, the college's students and teachers population increased and as such building extensions were made to hold the entire students capacity. And till date Our population keeps increasing and increasing.
The Present times of G.B.H.S Bafoussam
Now, the fresh moment of LYBIBAF can still be seen as the environment and its plants are much more older and still maintains it's beauty. Time has passed and passed, and finally the permanent beauty of the college has been attained. Also, thanks to the honoured x-principals till date for great works within our school campus.
